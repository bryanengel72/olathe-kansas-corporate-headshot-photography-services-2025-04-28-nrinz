Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical session take?

Most individual sessions last 30-45 minutes, including time for outfit changes and multiple poses. We ensure you get a variety of shots without feeling rushed.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors. Bring 2-3 outfit options for variety.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your professionally retouched photos within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer group corporate rates?

Yes, we offer special rates for corporate teams of 5 or more. Contact us for custom packages and on-site shooting options.

What is your rescheduling policy?

We understand that plans change. You can reschedule up to 48 hours before your session at no additional cost. Late changes may incur a fee.
